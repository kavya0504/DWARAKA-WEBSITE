Dwarka, once called Anarta and Kusasthali, is believed to have been built by Lord Vishwakarma on the request of Lord Krishna. The golden city was submerged into the sea after Krishnaâ€™s departure. It was the capital of the Yadava dynasty, including Krishna, Balarama, and others.

Archaeological Discoveries

Marine excavations since 1963 have uncovered underwater walls, anchors, and artifacts. These findings suggest Dwarka was a thriving ancient port and possibly the same city mentioned in the Mahabharata.

Medieval to Modern Era
